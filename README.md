Gameplay Premise:
1. REGISTER with your own prefereed username and password
2. When account created, input your data and press LOGIN to enter the game!
3. In the game, CLICK on red candies and gold candies to increase your streak and score!
4. BEWARE of evil-candies, which explode, destroying good candies around them, and also take lives from your total lives pool. 
5. Do NOT let the good candies escape from the screen, or you'll lose the life!
6. Practice until your aim is GODLY.
